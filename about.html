Our Story

My nephew loves cookies so much that we can't even say the word around him without him getting excited and acting up. To improve my frontend skills, starting with HTML and CSS, I've created a mock cookie business website themed around his love for cookies. Of all cookies, we recommend the "brown cookie," also known as the Oreo, as my nephew says. This project not only helps me practice my web development skills but also brings a fun and personal touch to my learning experience.
